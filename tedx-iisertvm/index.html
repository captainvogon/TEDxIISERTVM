About the event

With a carefully curated lineup of 8 speakers, each offering a distinct perspective, TEDxIISERTVM will be a one-day event scheduled for the 10th of March, 2024.

The theme for this year's TEDx event is “The Ripple Effect'', with the tagline “Greatness from small beginnings”, and it resonates with our ethos of fostering insightful conversations that propel transformative thinking. In the context of our event, it signifies the cumulative effect of diverse perspectives converging to create a powerful wave of inspiration. As we envision it, each speaker at TEDxIISERTVM contributes a unique drop to this collective ripple, fostering an intellectual current that resonates long after the event concludes.

Organized with a vision to amplify voices of brilliance and curiosity, TEDxIISERTVM is an event that will converge diverse perspectives, unlocking the power of transformative ideas. It might be one of these ideas that has the power to reshape the narrative of your progress.

About TEDx


In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program for local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TEDTalks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection. These local, self-organized events are branded with TEDx, where x = an independently organized TED event. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events are self-organized.
